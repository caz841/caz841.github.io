HI Meetup Framework

You can check out but you can never leave. Keep the flame alive in your city with this easy to implement Hyper Meetup framework

Step 1

Find fellow Hypers in your city

Step 2

Connect with your fellow Hypers (keep a record of who you've connected with you'll need this next)

Step 3

Choose a venue and date for your first meet up

Step 4

Send a LinkedIn message to you new contacts inviting them to their first Meetup (between 4 & 8 participants is ideal)
