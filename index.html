Meet & Play the game with a cool name

You can check out but you can never leave. Keep the flame alive in your city with this easy to implement Hyper Meetup framework.

Connect

The first thing you need to do is find some like minded Hypers near you and connect with them. Try using our city search or just run round the streets shouting "Check In". See what works best.

Once you've found some Hypers make sure you note down who you've found so you can invite them later (or stalk them ruthlessly, whatever floats your boat).

City Search

Find fellow Hypers in your city

Gather

Find a cool venue and an evening your heaving social schedule isn't full. Ping all your new found friends a quick message letting them know and wait for the replies to come flooding in!

We recommend between 4 & 8 people is an ideal number to make the event run smoothly with teams of 3 or 4 people. If you're struggling for numbers why not invite some like minded friends along to share the Hyper techniques.

Checklist

To help you prepare we've created a checklist to download.

It contains a list of all the supplies you'll need, timings for each part of the evening and a handy print out of templates you might need.

  Download checklist

Play

Now it's time for the fun to begin - you can play the game with no name. It has been specially designed to help you flex your creative muscles and remind you of the good times when you were in the Hyper bubble.

To get you started download the card pack and print it / cut it out. If you find your meetups are going really well you might want to invest in one of the custom made cards sets - they really are amazing - honest!

Repeat

Get some feedback off your group see how great they all think you are. Then take all the lovely feedback on board in true Hyper style and hack the meeting.

Get the next date and time in and do it all again!
